Instructions

  1. Knock all the blocks off the screen before the timer get to zero
  2. Refresh webpage to restart game
  3. All ball objects and cubes can be clicked and moved with the mouse

  4. Bird Controls:

    • Click and drag the red ball, let go to fire
    • Click on bird and throw with mouse

    • Spawn Birds:

      • R button resets the red ball
      • B button spawns balls at mouse location

  5. Propeller Controls:

    Every button press adds speed in the direction pressed

    • arrow Left spins prop Clockwise
    • arrow Right spins prop Counterclockwise
